About Me

Hi, I'm Furkan Kus, a world-class athlete and sports science specialist. My athletic journey began in Turkey, where I won numerous national and international medals in bocce. In 2019, I achieved two top-five finishes at the Bocce World Championship, and in 2024, I proudly represented the USA Bocce National Team at the World Championship. Academically, I hold a degree from Hacettepe University‚Äôs Faculty of Sports Sciences, specializing in athletic performance, movement science, and flexibility training. I am also a NASM-certified personal trainer, focusing on flexibility, mobility, and sports performance. My goal is to help clients improve muscle balance, reduce injury risks, and build stronger, more resilient bodies. While working as a Flexologist at StretchLab, I received specialized training in stretch therapy and movement correction. I have developed professional-level expertise in the neck region, helping everyone from office workers to athletes relieve neck tension, improve posture, and enhance overall movement quality. My mission is to combine my international athletic background with scientific training to guide people of all ages toward healthier, stronger, and more balanced lives. By sharing my experience, I aim to elevate both the performance and quality of life of my clients..
